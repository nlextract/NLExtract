3D Apps with Dutch Open GeoData

by: Just van den Broecke

Various web apps to demonstrate and use 3D web mapping. Sources for these apps in NLExtract GitHub.

OpenTopo with CesiumJS

OpenTopo in Cesium Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS

Ditto with Relief map from AHN2 - viewer with AHN2 Relief Map tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS

Historic Map in Cesium Viewer - viewer with Bonnebladen 1925 tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS

Top10NL3D with CesiumJS

Dutch topography 3D vector. Transformed from ESRI FGDB to GeoJSON. See conversion tools.

Top10NL3D on OpenTopo in Cesium Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km and 3DBuildings from Top10NL3D (Valkenburg)

Top10NL3D on OpenTopo in OpenLayers3+Cesium Integrated - ditto, but using OL3-Cesium integration

Navigation in examples: 


    Panning
    Mouse Left click-hold + drag

    Zooming
    Right click-hold + drag, or
    Mouse wheel scroll

    Rotating/tilting
    Middle click-hold + drag, or
    CTRL + Left click-hold + drag
